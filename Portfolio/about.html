I am James Plete, a Web Developer based in Valenzuela City, Philippines. I'm currently a sophomore pursuing Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela.

Whenever I have free time, I would dedicate myself to learn new things, not just in tech, but also in other aspects of my life. I believe that improving oneself starts with learning new things and gaining new perspectives about the world. That's why I believe that as long we're alive, we can always improve ourselves through perseverance and effort.

While learning and builidng things produces good results overtime, we should also not turn a blind eye to stress and burnouts. For me, going into nature trips and spending time with my family and friends helps reduce these. The greeneries and the air soothes my mind and helps me generate new ideas and solutions.

Like any other people navigating through their life though, it's natural that we encounter problems and hardships here and there. There would be some time in our lives that there is seemingly no hope anymore, but it is very important not to give up and trust yourself that all things will be alright.

Finally, I believe that we should always seek challenges and leave our comfort zones to explore more about life. Doing so would not only let us grow as an individual but would also teach us the things we want and value in our lives. Just a reminder not to treat ourselves harshly during the process. Padayon!
